Forbidden Packs

Resource packs/client side mods that provide an unfair advantage

Why These Are Banned:

The following packs are not allowed on the server and will result in a ban if used.

They are banned because they provide unfair advantages or disrupt the gameplay experience.

Banned Resource Packs/Client-Side Mods:

  • Any pack that provides an unfair advantage (e.g., X-Ray packs or fullbright).
  • Packs that alter the game's mechanics in a way that is not intended (e.g, freelook).
  • Client-side mods that automate gameplay or provide unfair advantages (e.g, Wurst Client).

Banned packs include—but are not limited to:

  • Fullbright
  • Gamma
  • X-Ray
  • Freelook
  • Wurst Client
  • Red Trapped Chests
  • No More Pumpkin Blur
  • Better Clear Water
  • Better invisibility particles
  • Swing Through Grass
  • Better Placement
  • Ungrab Mouse Mod
  • Faster Ladder Climbing

Consequences of Using These Mods:

Using any of the above packs or mods will result in a kick/ban from the server.

Players are expected to play fairly and respect the rules of the server.
