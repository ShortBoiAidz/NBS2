NoodleBox SMP Laws

There aren't many mandatory laws to follow all throughout the server. Most laws are created by each faction.

How the Laws Work:

Each faction can create its own set of laws that must be followed by its members.

These laws are enforced by the faction leaders or court and are usually listed in a book and quill in the town square.

However, there are some universal laws that apply to all players on the server, regardless of factions.


Universal Laws:

  • No hacking or cheating of any kind.
    • This includes resource packs — see forbidden resource packs

  • No spamming or advertising in chat.
    • This includes using the /msg command to spam other players.

  • No entering the Slimeworld biome (commonly known as "No Man's Land").
    • Entering this biome crashes the whole server due to lag — thanks Liam.

  • No Loopholes
    • Wording of the laws is not important as long as the message gets across.
    • Anyone who abuses loopholes in the system will be kicked/banned.
